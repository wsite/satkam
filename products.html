Black Tea Choose Whats Best For You

Black tea is one of the most popular type of tea. One of the reason for this is that black tea has longer storage life and has strong flavor compared to less oxidized tea. We have two types of Black Tea depending upon your mood.

  • SF Floral

    SF Floral is made from regular tea leaves of the plantation. It is made with unique techenique different from the standard orthodox tea procedure. It is widely used for regular use and regular consumption. SF Floral provides unique burst of flavor in economic price range without any compromise in the quality.

  • HR Floral

    HR Floral is made from special tea leaves of the plantation. HR Floral is made by special process in which the tea leaves are carefully hand picked from the bushes and then goes through process of withering, rolling, fermenting, drying, sorting and packing. The tea leaves are carefully chosen and specially made so that you get the richness of taste while you enjoy your black tea.

Green Tea Special type for special needs

The tea leaves are carefully picked and then gently steamed and roasted after whtich they are rolled, dried, sorted and packed. Green tea are less processed types of tea and made from unoxidized tea leaves. Our green tea are flavorful and packed with anti-oxidants. It has various kinds of benefits to the body. We currently have three types of green tea available.

  • Guranse Green Tea

    Guranse Green Tea is for the green tea lovers. It offers the fresh and subtle taste of the green tea. It can be used as the regular green tea because its comparitively cheaper and provides the subtle taste of green tea.

  • Emerald Green Tea

    Emerald Green Tea are prepared for those who prefer a lush taste of the green tea. It provides a little more hint in the taste than the regular Guranse Green Tea.

  • Mystic Green Tea

    Mystic Green Tea are made from hand plucked tea from the tea plantation. It is carefully prepared and provides the best experience of the green tea. It provides a strong taste of the green tea with all the health benefits as well.
